About Us

Guitar Shop Baneshwore Kathmandu is a popular destination for guitar enthusiasts in Nepal's capital city. The shop offers a wide range of guitars, including acoustic, electric, and bass guitars, as well as accessories such as picks, strings, and cases. The staff is knowledgeable and friendly, always willing to offer advice and help customers find the perfect guitar to suit their needs and budget.
